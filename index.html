Rotaract Project

Rotaract District 3220

Rotaract is a global youth movement recognized and elevated as a membership type of Rotary International that empowers youth to lead positive change in communities. Introduced in 1969 to Sri Lanka, Rotaract is one of the largest voluntary youth organizations led by proactive and dynamic individuals in the country above the age of 18 years. Since the inception, it has evolved as a strong youth-led platform nurturing fellowship and leadership for social innovation in the country. Presently, Rotaract in Rotary International District 3220 Sri Lanka and Maldives comprises more than 7000 reported members from 70+ Rotaract clubs with a geographical representation in 7 administrative provinces of the country. The movement carries a locational significance in the communities of Colombo, Negombo, Kandy, Kurunegala, Hatton, Rathnapura, Badulla, and Jaffna due to its steady growth in membership. Furthermore, I was given the opportunity to help revamp the 3220 Homepage and give it a fresh new look.
